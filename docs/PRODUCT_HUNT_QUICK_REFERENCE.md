# Product Hunt Launch: Quick Reference

## The Playbook (One Page)

**Timeline**: 4 weeks of prep → Launch day → 4 weeks momentum

---

## Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–4)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Pick launch date (Tue–Thu, 12 AM PT)
- [ ] Audit product (mobile responsive, demo works, CTA clear)
- [ ] Record 45-sec demo video
- [ ] Create demo account (pre-loaded with data)

### Week 2: Social Proof
- [ ] Get 3–5 testimonials (with photos)
- [ ] Create 7 polished screenshots
- [ ] Write killer description (use formula below)
- [ ] Prepare video (optional but recommended)

### Week 3: Community
- [ ] Reach out to 50+ people (founders, VCs, your users)
- [ ] Join PH community (comment on 5–10 products)
- [ ] Draft all comments (5 staggered comments, ready to post)
- [ ] Prepare email + social templates

### Week 4: Final Prep
- [ ] Set up PH product (schedule, not published)
- [ ] Soft-test with 5 people, get feedback
- [ ] Refine based on feedback
- [ ] Set phone reminders for launch day

---

## Description Formula (Copy-Paste)

**Headline** (60 chars): 
`"Learn to Code, Earn Money, Rank Up"`

**Tagline** (one sentence):
`"Like LeetCode meets Upwork meets your coding bootcamp — with an AI mentor."`

**Description** (4 paragraphs):

1. **Problem**: "Junior developers face a choice: learn without income (bootcamp) or earn without learning (freelance). We solve both."

2. **Solution**: "Adventurers Guild is a gamified platform where developers complete real coding projects, earn money, and rank up (F → S) with AI guidance. Companies get production code. Developers get job-ready experience."

3. **What's New**: "We just launched AI Copilot: contextual hints + upcoming chat mentor powered by Claude. This is unique—learn while doing real work."

4. **CTA**: "Join 100+ developers and 50+ companies building real stuff. Sign up free, accept a quest, start earning."

## Comment Strategy (5 Staggered Comments)

**Comment 1** (12:15 AM): Founder intro
- Who you are
- Why you built this
- What problem it solves
- CTA: "Ask me anything!"

**Comment 2** (1–2 hours later): Product deep dive
- Explain the AI Copilot (Phase 1, 2, 3)
- Why it's unique
- How it works

**Comment 3** (3–4 hours later): Social proof
- Metrics (100 users, 50 companies, $20K paid)
- Traction (4.2★ rating)
- What's launching today

**Comment 4** (5–6 hours later): Founder story
- Personal journey (why you built this)
- The "19, first-time founder" angle
- Vision for the future

**Comment 5** (ongoing): Respond to questions
- Every comment gets a response in first 6 hours
- Be grateful, be genuine, be helpful

## Common Mistakes

❌ Post product, then disappear
❌ Over-hype in description
❌ Forget live demo link
❌ Ignore comments
❌ Launch on Friday
❌ Post link everywhere at once
❌ Use clichés ("Revolutionary," "Disrupting")

---

## The Secret: Engagement

**Most important**: Be present and genuine.

- Respond to EVERY comment in first 6 hours
- Thank people who upvote
- Answer questions thoughtfully
- Share your real story
- Show you care

**This is what gets you Top 5.** Not just the product, but the founder.

---

## Phase 1 MVP Timeline (Important!)

To have the strongest PH launch, do this:

- **This Friday**: Ship Phase 1 MVP
- **Week 2**: Beta test with 10 users
- **Week 3**: Refine + collect testimonials
- **Week 4**: Launch on Product Hunt

Why? Real testimonials + metrics = stronger launch = better rank.
