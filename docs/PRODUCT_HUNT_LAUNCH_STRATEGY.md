# Product Hunt Launch Strategy: Adventurers Guild

## Overview

**Goal**: Land in **Top 5** on Product Hunt launch day (ideally #1 or #2)

**Why it matters**: 
- 50K+ eyeballs on day 1
- VCs monitor PH launches actively
- Bootcamp founders check PH
- Media coverage (TechCrunch, ProductHunt's newsletter)
- Founder credibility spike

**Timeline**: 4 weeks of prep → Launch → 4 weeks momentum

---

## Pre-Launch Strategy (Weeks 1–4 Before Launch)

### Week 1: Foundation & Audit

#### Task 1: Choose Your Launch Date
- **Best days**: Tuesday–Thursday (avoid Monday chaos, Friday fatigue)
- **Best time**: 12:01 AM PT (midnight PH resets)
- **Avoid**: Holidays, major tech events (Apple event, Google I/O), competing launches
- **Check**: ProductHunt.com/timeline to see what's launching

**Decision**: Pick a date 4 weeks out. Lock it. Build to that date.

#### Task 2: Audit Your Product for PH
- ✅ **Mobile responsive**: Test on iPhone (most PH voters use mobile)
- ✅ **First 10 seconds**: Does someone understand what you do in 10 seconds?
- ✅ **Call-to-action**: Is it obvious how to try/signup?
- ✅ **Screenshots**: Do they show the product, not just marketing?
- ✅ **Video demo**: Optional but highly recommended (30–60 seconds)

#### Task 3: Prepare Demo Environment
- ✅ Create a demo account (pre-loaded with quests + data)
- ✅ Record a 45-second video showing: Quest board → Hints sidebar → Accept quest
- ✅ Make sure demo link is 1-click away (no login required)
- ✅ Test it 10 times on different browsers

**Deliverable by end of Week 1**: Confirmed launch date, clean product demo

---

### Week 2: Social Proof & Content

#### Task 1: Gather Testimonials (3–5)
- Email your 10 most active adventurers + 5 companies
- Ask: "In one sentence, how has Adventurers Guild helped you?"
- Get their photo/name (makes testimonials credible)

**Example prompts**:
- "This is like [similar product] but for developers"
- "I learned [skill] while earning $[amount]"
- "The hints sidebar helped me 3x faster"

#### Task 2: Create Screenshot Sequence (5–7 images)
- **Screenshot 1**: Full quest board (shows the "market")
- **Screenshot 2**: Quest detail page with hints sidebar (THE copilot feature!)
- **Screenshot 3**: Dashboard with earnings + XP progression
- **Screenshot 4**: Company perspective (posting a quest)
- **Screenshot 5**: Leaderboard (social proof, competition)
- **Screenshot 6**: Stats dashboard (traction: users, quests, money paid)
- **Screenshot 7**: Mobile view (responsive proof)

**Annotation style**: Clean, no clutter. Use arrows/circles to highlight key features.

#### Task 3: Write Your PH Description

**Format**:
```
Headline (60 chars max):
"Learn to Code, Earn Money, Rank Up"

Tagline (one sentence):
"Like LeetCode meets Upwork meets your coding bootcamp — with an AI mentor."

Full Description (3–4 paragraphs, 200 words):

Paragraph 1: Problem (What pain do we solve?)
"Junior developers face a choice: learn without income (bootcamp) or earn 
without learning (freelance platforms). We solve both."

Paragraph 2: Solution (What do we do?)
"Adventurers Guild is a gamified platform where developers complete real 
coding projects, earn money, and rank up (F → S) with AI-powered guidance. 
Companies get production code. Developers get job-ready experience."

Paragraph 3: What's special (Why now?)
"We just launched AI Copilot: contextual hints + upcoming chat mentor 
powered by Claude. This is unique—you learn while doing real work."

Paragraph 4: Call-to-action (What do you want?)
"Join 100+ developers and 50+ companies building real stuff. Sign up free, 
accept a quest, and start learning while you earn."
```

**Key phrases for PH**:
- "AI-powered"
- "Learn while you earn"
- "Real projects"
- "Gamified" (PH loves gamification)
- "For developers"
- "Unique" (PH voters hate "Uber for X", love "The [comparison]")

**Deliverable by end of Week 2**: 3+ testimonials, 7 screenshots, polished description

### Week 3: Community Setup & Seeding

#### Task 1: Build Your Maker Network
Before launch day, you need people to upvote immediately (first 2–4 hours are critical).

**Who to reach out to**:
- Your 100+ existing adventurers (email list)
- Twitter followers (especially founder/dev accounts)
- LinkedIn connections (especially VCs, bootcamp founders)
- Tech communities: Dev.to, Indie Hackers, Reddit (/r/startups, /r/webdev)
- Mentor/advisor network
- ProductHunt community (join discussions, comment on products, build karma)

**How to reach out** (2 weeks before launch):
```
Email subject: "We're launching on Product Hunt soon — help us out?"

Body:
Hi [Name],

We're launching Adventurers Guild on [Date] at 12 AM PT on Product Hunt.

It's a platform where developers earn money while learning to code. 
Think LeetCode + Upwork + bootcamp in one.

We'd love your support on launch day. If you like it, just upvote 
here: [PH link]

Let me know if you have questions!

-[Your name]
```

**Send this to**:
- Existing user list: personalized
- Twitter/LinkedIn: template is fine
- Communities: post once, mention launch

#### Task 2: Join ProductHunt Community Early
- Create account (if not already)
- Comment thoughtfully on 5–10 product launches
- Upvote things you genuinely like
- Build karma (PH users notice)
- Follow makers you admire

**Why?** PH community respects people who engage. You'll get better reception.

#### Task 3: Prepare Launch Day Communications
Create templates for:
- **PH comment response** (to questions during launch day)
- **Twitter thread** (announce launch, link to PH)
- **Email to users** ("Check us out on PH, upvote if you like it!")
- **Slack/Discord messages** (to maker communities)
- **Hacker News link** (if applicable)

**Deliverable by end of Week 3**: 50+ people tagged for launch day, templates ready

### Week 4: Final Prep & Soft Launch

#### Task 1: Set Up PH Product (Not Published Yet)
- Create ProductHunt account
- Fill out all fields (tagline, description, screenshots)
- Upload video (if you have it)
- Schedule for your launch date at 12:01 AM PT

**Do NOT publish yet.** This is just a draft/schedule.

#### Task 2: Soft Launch (Ask for Feedback)
- Show your PH page to 5 trusted people
- Get feedback: "Is this compelling? Would you click upvote?"
- Refine description, screenshots, video based on feedback
- Test the demo link one more time

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't just post product, then disappear**
- People vote when maker is PRESENT and engaging
- Be on PH from 12 AM → 11 PM PT on launch day

❌ **Don't over-hype in description**
- "Revolutionary" → Good people skip
- Show, don't tell. Let screenshots speak

❌ **Don't forget the demo**
- Link to live demo should be 1 click away
- Make sure it loads fast (test from mobile)

❌ **Don't ignore comments**
- Respond to EVERY comment in first 6 hours
- Even if it's "thanks!" just say "thanks right back!"

❌ **Don't post your link everywhere at once**
- Spread outreach over 2 weeks (looks more natural)
- Focus on quality: targeted communities beat spam

❌ **Don't launch on Friday**
- Weekend upvotes drop off
- Launch Tue–Thu for sustained momentum

❌ **Don't ignore data**
- Track what people are asking
- If 5 people ask about pricing, add FAQ
- If 10 people want demo link, make it bigger
