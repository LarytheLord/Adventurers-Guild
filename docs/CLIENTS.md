# Clients & Partner Organizations

Welcome to **The Adventurers Guild** — where organizations post real-world coding quests and connect with skilled student developers.

---

## 🏢 What Is The Adventurers Guild?

The Adventurers Guild is a gamified educational marketplace that connects organizations with motivated computer science students. We call our projects **"Quests"** — real coding tasks commissioned by companies and completed by students ("Adventurers") who earn XP, skill points, rankings, and monetary rewards.

### Why Partner With Us?

- **Access Pre-vetted Talent**: Students progress through ranks (F to S) based on completed work quality
- **Cost-Effective Development**: Pay competitive rates for quality work
- **Real-World Impact**: Students work on production code, not toy projects
- **Gamified Quality**: Rank system ensures motivated, skilled developers
- **Mediated QA**: Open Paws team reviews all submissions before delivery

---

## 🎯 How It Works for Organizations

### 1. **Register Your Organization**
Create a company account at [adventurersguild.vercel.app/register](https://adventurersguild.vercel.app/register)

### 2. **Post a Quest**
Describe your project with:
- Clear requirements and deliverables
- Required skills (React, Node.js, Python, etc.)
- Difficulty rank (F = beginner, S = expert)
- Budget and timeline
- XP and monetary rewards

### 3. **Review Applications**
Adventurers apply to your quest. Review their:
- Current rank (F, E, D, C, B, A, S)
- Skill badges
- Quest completion history
- Portfolio submissions

### 4. **Select & Collaborate**
Choose the right adventurer(s) for your quest. Work together through:
- Integrated DevSync development environment
- Progress tracking dashboards
- Direct communication channels

### 5. **Review & Approve**
Submit work goes through QA review:
- **Open Paws mediation**: All submissions reviewed before reaching you
- **Quality assurance**: Human QA ensures work meets standards
- **Revision support**: If work needs improvement, we coordinate revisions

### 6. **Payment & Completion**
Once approved:
- Adventurer receives XP, skill points, and payment
- Your quest is marked complete
- Leave feedback to help the adventurer's reputation

## 💰 Quest Pricing Guidelines

Quest rewards vary by difficulty and complexity:

| Rank | Difficulty | Typical XP | Monetary Range | Example Projects |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------------|------------------|
| **F** | Beginner | 50-100 XP | $25 - $75 | Bug fixes, small features, documentation |
| **E** | Elementary | 100-200 XP | $75 - $150 | Simple components, API integrations |
| **D** | Developing | 200-350 XP | $150 - $300 | Full features, medium complexity |
| **C** | Competent | 350-500 XP | $300 - $600 | Complex features, multi-file changes |
| **B** | Skilled | 500-750 XP | $600 - $1,200 | System design, architecture work |
| **A** | Advanced | 750-1,000 XP | $1,200 - $2,500 | Full modules, production systems |
| **S** | Expert | 1,000+ XP | $2,500+ | Critical infrastructure, complex integrations |

**Platform Commission**: 15% fee covers payment processing, QA mediation, and platform maintenance.

## 🛡️ Quality Assurance & Mediation

**Open Paws** serves as the trusted intermediary between organizations and adventurers:

### Our QA Process:
1. **Submission Review**: All work reviewed by technical QA team
2. **Quality Check**: Code quality, functionality, requirements compliance
3. **Feedback Loop**: If revisions needed, we coordinate with adventurer
4. **Final Delivery**: Only approved work reaches your organization

### Benefits:
- **No direct conflict**: We handle all dispute resolution
- **Consistent quality**: Human review on every submission
- **Risk mitigation**: Pay only for work that meets standards

## 🎓 Special Programs

### Intern Track
For organizations seeking longer-term relationships:
- **Squad-based delivery**: Teams of 3-5 students led by a Party Leader
- **D-rank+ quests**: More complex, sustained projects
- **Mentorship included**: Senior developers guide teams
- **Pipeline to hiring**: Evaluate talent for full-time roles

### Bootcamp Track
Intensive, short-term projects:
- **Pair programming**: Teams of 2 students
- **E-rank quests**: Focused, well-scoped work
- **Fast turnaround**: 2-4 week completion cycles
- **High engagement**: Full-time student commitment

### Hackathon Continuation
Turn hackathon prototypes into production:
- **First-refusal window**: Hackathon teams get exclusive access to continuation quests
- **7-day exclusivity**: Before quest opens to public board
- **Seamless transition**: Continue where hackathon left off
